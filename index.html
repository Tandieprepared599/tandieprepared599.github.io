☕ lungo - Keep your computer awake easily

Download Lungo for Windows

Lungo helps you manage your system power settings. It sits in your system tray and forces your computer to stay awake. You do not need to change your Windows power settings every time you want to prevent sleep.

💡 Why use Lungo

Windows computers often turn off the display or enter sleep mode to save energy. This causes problems during long downloads, presentations, or while watching media. Lungo acts as an inhibitor to tell Windows that your system should remain active. The application runs quietly in your background, which keeps your workflow smooth.

🛠️ System Requirements

To use this application, your computer needs the following:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • At least 50 MB of free hard drive space
  • Standard user permissions on your computer

📥 How to install

Follow these steps to set up the software on your machine:

  1. Visit the official release page to see the latest versions.
  2. Locate the file ending in .exe in the Assets section of the latest release.
  3. Click the file to download it to your computer.
  4. Find the file in your Downloads folder and double-click it to start the program.
  5. You may see a message from Windows Defender. Click More Info and then Run Anyway if you trust the software source.

⚙️ Usage

Once the program runs, you will see a small cup icon in your system tray near your clock. Right-click this icon to see the options. You can toggle the sleep prevention feature on or off with one click. When the feature is active, your system will not enter sleep mode automatically. When you want your computer to use normal power settings again, turn the feature off or close the app.

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Lungo runs locally on your computer. It does not track your data or send information over the internet. The program only communicates with your internal Windows settings to prevent idle timers. It requires no elevated administrator rights to function for your user account.

❓ Troubleshooting

If the icon does not appear, check your hidden system tray icons by clicking the small upward arrow near your clock. If you start a new session and the app does not load, ensure you have the correct version for your Windows OS. If you accidentally close the app, simply run the .exe file again to restore the functionality.
